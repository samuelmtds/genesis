To build genesis xdoclet module, you need:

- Ant (http://ant.apache.org/)
- XDoclet 1.2.2 source distribution (http://xdoclet.sourceforge.net/)

Copy genesis module to xdoclet modules dir (<XDOCLET_HOME>/modules/genesis)

Go to <XDOCLET_HOME>/modules and type:

   ant modules
   
xdoclet-genesis-module-1.2.2.jar will be created (<XDOCLET_HOME>/target/lib)
